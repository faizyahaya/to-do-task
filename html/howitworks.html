How To-Do Task Works

We make it easy to connect clients with skilled local service providers for everyday tasks.

Get Things Done in 3 Simple Steps

1. Post or Browse Tasks

Clients post tasks or browse categories. Providers view available jobs in their area.

2. Chat & Confirm

Clients and providers communicate, agree on scope, price, and timing.

3. Complete & Pay

Once the task is done, the provider gets paid. Everyone can leave a review.
